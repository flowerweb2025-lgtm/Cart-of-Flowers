Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across all seven Emirates in the UAE. Same-day delivery is available in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah when orders are placed before 2 PM.

How fresh are your flowers?

All our flowers are sourced fresh daily from local and international growers. We guarantee the freshest blooms with maximum vase life.

Can I customize my bouquet?

Absolutely! Contact us with your specific requirements, and our expert florists will create a custom arrangement just for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and all major digital payment methods for your convenience.

Do you offer corporate services?

Yes! We provide corporate floral services including office arrangements, event decoration, and bulk orders for businesses.

What is your refund policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours and we'll make it right with a replacement or full refund.
